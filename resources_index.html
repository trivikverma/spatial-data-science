A guide to Critical Data Science#

This comprehensive guide is a work-in-progress manifesto for approaching data science with a critical lens. We are in no way objective beings. Our work always stems from our positionality and its intersection with our morality and agendas. This document helps us learn about how to acknowledge ourselves as part of the research and practice processes, and learn to become better programmers, data scientists and policymakers.
